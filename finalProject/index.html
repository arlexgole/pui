NEPAL

Gem of a Country

Nepal is a landlocked country between India and China. It is located in the Himalaya and home to the eight of the world's ten tallest mountains (including Mount Everest).

General Info

Capital: Kathmandhu

Official Language: Nepali

Population: ~ 28 million

Places to Visit

Tourism is the largest industry in Nepal and makes up the largest source of foreign exchange and revenue. The mountains make it a hotspot for people seeking adventure. There also important religious pilgrimage sites throughout Nepal, such as Lumbini (the birthplace of Gautama Buddha).

01 - Nagarkot

Nagarkot offers a panoramic view of the Kathmandu Vallue, making it a popular hiking route for tourists.

02 - Gokyo Lake

Located at an altitude of 15,000+ feet above sea laeve, these lakes are the world's highest freshwater lake system.

03 - Boudhanath

The Stupa is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the largest stupas in the world.

04 - Pokhara

Pokhara is the second largest city in Nepal and considered the tourism capital of Nepal.

05 - Everest

Also known as Sagarmatha, it is the Earth's highesht mountain above sea level at 29,029 ft.

Food

Momo

A traditional delicacy in Nepal. One of the most popular fast foods.

Dal bhat

A traditional meal consisting of steamed rice, vegetables, and lentil soup.

Thukpa

One of the Indo-Tibetan food, having great influence both from Tibet and from South Asia.
